Eligibility Criteria for Meal Requests / Membership:

MerryMeal serves qualified adults living at home who are unable to cook for themselves or maintain their nutritional status due to age, illness, disability, or other circumstances. Eligible individuals may include:

  • Elderly Adults: Senior citizens who may face challenges with cooking or have limited mobility.
  • Individuals with Disabilities: Those with physical or cognitive disabilities that impact their ability to prepare meals independently.
  • Chronically Ill: Individuals managing chronic illnesses or undergoing treatments that affect their ability to cook.
  • Recovering Patients: People recovering from surgeries or medical procedures who require temporary meal assistance.
  • Low-Income Families: Families facing financial hardship who struggle to afford nutritious meals for themselves or their loved ones.
